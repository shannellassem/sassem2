Project Proposal

Concept

Pawfect Journey is a website/app that’s designed to help new/experienced pet owners help them through various stages of their pets life, give advice, set reminders, and keep track of milestones and events.

Goal

Aiding pet owners from adoption to the rest of their lives. It helps make the process of obtaining and owning a pet stress-free.

What's Included

Personalized pet profiles

  • Users can input their pet’s breed, age, adoption history, and medical history.

Resources

  • Get access to videos, articles, tips on training, nutrition, grooming, and health.
  • Quick access tips for urgent pet situations.

Milestone tracker

  • Depending on age, keeps track of key moments or behaviorism habits pets may have.

Reminders

  • Notifications for vet appointments, vaccinations, and medications
