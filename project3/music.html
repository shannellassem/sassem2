Music

Avril Lavigne is known for her rebellious spirit, pop-punk anthems, and emotional ballads that have connected with fans around the world for over two decades. From her early 2000s hits to her latest releases, Avril’s music continues to evolve while staying true to her signature style. Below is a look at her incredible discography — albums that shaped a generation and continue to inspire fans everywhere.
