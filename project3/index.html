About Avril Lavigne

Avril Lavigne is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actress known for her rebellious style and pop-punk sound. She was born on September 27, 1984, in Belleville, Ontario, Canada. Avril first rose to fame in 2002 with her debut album Let Go, which featured hit songs like Complicated and Sk8er Boi. Throughout her career, Avril has sold over 40 million albums worldwide and is often called the “Pop Punk Queen.” Her music blends punk, rock, and pop, inspiring a generation of fans with her edgy style and honest lyrics. Avril is also known for her fashion influence, especially with her iconic tie-wearing look from the early 2000s.
